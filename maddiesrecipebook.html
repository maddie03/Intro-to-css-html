Recipe #1

  • Time: 1h 5mins
  • Serves: 6 people
Ingredients Quantity

Step 1: Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Step 2:Melt butter in a medium saucepan over medium heat. Saute onion for 2 minutes. Stir in flour and cook 1 minute, stirring constantly. Stir in milk, salt, mustard and pepper; cook, stirring frequently, until mixture boils and thickens.
Step 3:Meanwhile, bring a pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add macaroni and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
